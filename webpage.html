Welcome to
Crown Oasis
The CROWN OASIS in Singapore is the country's first hotel to be located next to a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Singapore's UNESCO PARK.

This hotel welcomes you as we write with you a new chapter and inject a new life into the hospitality sector of Singapore.

The hotel focuses on minimalistic and modern vibes that are brought to life via green technologies, biophilic design, iconic architecture, eco-friendliness, and lifestyle experiences, catering to aware and thoughtful travelers.




Whether for business or pleasure, take solace at one of Singapore's top hotels, with over 500 guest rooms with balconies and stunning views of the oasis garden.
